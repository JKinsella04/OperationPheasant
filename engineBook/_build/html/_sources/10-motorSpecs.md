# V5 Motor Specifications

## 100 RPM Motor Cartridge
- Stall torque of 2.1 N-M / 18.59 in lb
- 6:1 output gear ratio
- Output torque of 1.05 N-M / 9.3 in lb
- No load speed of 100rpm

- 18:1 output gear ratio
